CIVIL SOCIETY NIGHT


December, 7th - Open Government Partnership Paris Summit



Bringing together civic tech advocates

During the OGP summit, civil society organizations building civic tech tools from all over the world will come to Paris. In order to welcome and connect them altogether, we are throwing a party !

While head of states will be convening to the summit, the CSO Night will also be a chance to voice the values civil society organizations hold dear. Values that should drive civic technologies, with open source and open knowledge at their core.

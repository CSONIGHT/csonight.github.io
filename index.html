OPEN CIVIL SOCIETY NIGHT


December, 6th - Open Government Partnership Paris Summit



REGISTER



Bringing together civic tech activists

During the OGP summit, civil society organizations building civic tech tools from all over the world will come to Paris. In order to welcome and connect them altogether, we are throwing a party !

While heads of state will be convening at the summit, the CSO Night will be a chance for civil society organizations promoting civic technologies to voice their values : open source and open knowledge.

A series of lightning talks from civic tech leaders from all over the world will highlight those values. Do not hesitate to send your proposal !
